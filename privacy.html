Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026 · Terms of Service

This Privacy Policy describes how Solf.ai (“we”, “us”) processes personal data when you use our website and application (the “Service”). It should be read together with our Terms of Service.

1. Data controller

The controller of your personal data is the independent operator of Solf.ai, an individual entrepreneur / self-employed person established in the Russian Federation. Contact for privacy requests: @vwirex on Telegram.

2. Categories of data we process

  • Account & identity (Google Sign-In): when you choose to sign in with Google, we receive identifiers and profile elements that Google shares with our app (typically name, email address, profile picture URL) to display your account in the interface.
  • Messages & prompts: when you send a message, your text and a limited amount of prior conversation context are transmitted to our backend API so that an AI model can generate a reply. This is essential to provide the chat feature.
  • Images you attach: if you upload images for analysis, image data (e.g. encoded in the request) is sent to our backend for processing.
  • Local chat history & settings: chat threads and preferences (theme, language, font size, plan metadata, usage counters, etc.) may be stored in your browser using localStorage (and similar technologies) primarily on your device.
  • Cookie / storage consent: we record whether you accepted or declined the cookie banner, where applicable.
  • Technical & connection data: when you call our API, our hosting providers may process technical data such as IP address, timestamps, and request metadata for security, abuse prevention, and service operation.

3. Purposes and legal bases (including GDPR)

We process data to: operate and secure the Service; authenticate users; enforce usage limits and plans; comply with law; and communicate with you when you contact us. Where the EU/EEA/UK GDPR applies, legal bases may include: performance of a contract (providing the Service you request); legitimate interests (security, preventing fraud and abuse, improving reliability); consent where required (e.g. non-essential storage); and legal obligation where applicable.

4. Recipients and international transfers

We do not sell your personal data. We rely on infrastructure and service providers, which may include:

  • Google LLC (authentication);
  • Hosting / edge providers (e.g. Cloudflare or similar) that route and protect API traffic;
  • AI inference providers engaged by our backend to process prompts and return model outputs.

These providers may process data in countries other than your own (including the United States). Where required, transfers are safeguarded by appropriate mechanisms (such as standard contractual clauses offered by providers). You may request more detail about subprocessors by contacting us.

5. Retention

Data stored locally in your browser remains until you clear site data or use in-app controls. Server-side logs and processing are kept no longer than necessary for security, troubleshooting, and legal compliance—exact periods may depend on infrastructure defaults; contact us for specifics about your data.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, objection, and to withdraw consent where processing is consent-based. You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g. in your EU Member State of residence). To exercise rights, contact @vwirex. You can delete locally stored data by clearing your browser storage for our site.

7. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below the applicable minimum age. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures (such as HTTPS for API calls) to protect personal data. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9. Cookies and similar technologies

We use storage necessary for preferences and consent. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics cookies as described in our banner. See the cookie notice on first visit for choices where applicable.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy; the “Effective date” at the top will change. Material changes may be highlighted by updating this page.
